### EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The **SDEC Framework** resolves the long-standing conflict between orbital cleanup and national sovereignty. By shifting the legal and technical approach from "Ownership" to **"Environmental Intervention,"** we establish a safe, transparent, and economically viable pathway for total orbital remediation.

This repository serves as the public "Read-Only" headquarters for the SDEC protocol, detailing the regulatory framework, specialized fleet specifications, and the **Tokenized Trash** economic model.

### CORE PILLARS
1.  **Forensic Transparency:** Utilizing **MdEC** metadata standards to assign immutable **M-ID (Material Identity)** tokens to every retrieved asset.
2.  **Sovereign Security:** Implementing **Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKP)** to verify retrieval without breaching national security secrets.
3.  **Neutrality as Armor:** Hardware-locking GNC systems to ensure the fleet remains a strictly civil environmental utility.
4.  **Economic Sustainability:** Transforming orbital debris into a secondary market of tokenized materials.
