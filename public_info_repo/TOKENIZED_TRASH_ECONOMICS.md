# Economics of Orbital Excellence
## The Tokenized Trash Model

SDEC transforms orbital debris from a liability into a sustainable economic asset through the **Tokenized Trash** model.

---

### 1. M-ID Tokens
Metadata standards from the **Metadata Excellence Consortium (MdEC)** allow for a secondary market where companies buy and sell tokens representing salvaged materials.
*   **Asset Representation:** Every retrieved and sealed asset is assigned an **M-ID Token** (Mission/Material Identity).
*   **Ledger Integration:** These tokens are logged on an immutable global ledger, providing forensic proof of chain-of-custody.

### 2. Sovereign Subscription (Space Insurance)
Nations and operators pay **"Excellence Memberships"** to ensure orbital safety and compliance.
*   **The 10cm Standard:** Subscribers are guaranteed that any fragment larger than 10cm originating from their fleet will be recovered and forensicly tagged.
*   **Liability Buffer:** Membership reduces the premium of insurance and the geopolitical risk associated with debris collisions.

### 3. Universal Licensing
SDEC establishes an international **"Orbital Postal and Waste Service"** through technology licensing.
*   **Scale:** Blueprints for **Titaness Container Units (TCUs)** and M-ID tagging software are licensed to governments and private operators.
*   **Interoperability:** Ensures that any retrieval unit, regardless of origin, can interface with the SDEC grid and participate in the tokenized economy.
