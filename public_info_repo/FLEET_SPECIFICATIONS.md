# SDEC Specialized Fleet Specifications
## Active Remediation & Selective Harvesting

The SDEC fleet consists of specialized robotic units designed for the active removal of orbital threats.

---

### I. Vesta Scout (Identification)
The "Eyes" of the fleet. Vests Scouts perform high-resolution "Spectral Fingerprinting" to identify material and confirm COSPAR IDs.
*   **Sensor Suite:** LiDAR and Stereo-Optical sensors designed to navigate "dark," tumbling objects.
*   **Mission:** Identify high-risk, low-value debris (shrapnel, paint chips) for the "Sink" pipeline.

### II. Heavy Retrieval Hauler (Capture)
The "Hands" of the fleet. Captures high-mass debris (1–10+ tons). 
*   **Mechanism:** Synchronizes its rotation with complex tumbling objects before seizing them with the **"Titaness Reach"** robotic arm or tether systems.
*   **The Suicide Burn:** For high-risk remediation, a unit couples with a threat and exhausts its Delta-V to ensure both vaporize over unpopulated areas like Point Nemo.

### III. Stripper Craft (Dismantling)
Performs precision **"Micro-Gravitational Surgery"** to remove high-value components (solar arrays, batteries) without creating secondary debris.
*   **Mission:** Resource harvesting and high-fidelity salvage.

### IV. Titaness Container Unit (TCU)
Shielded, pressurized storage pods where captured debris is sealed.
*   **Tokenization:** Every TCU is "Tokenized" with a microscopic, cryptographic QR-code called an **M-ID**.
*   **Security:** Safeguards the contents for "Blind Verification" by international observers.

---

### II. Core Strategies
*   **The Sink:** Shrapnel and low-value threats are pushed into atmospheric re-entry for vaporization.
*   **The Source:** Large-scale assets (satellites, upper stages) are intercepted for controlled management and potential resource recycling.
