## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: THE VISION
The orbital environment has transitioned from a pristine frontier to a congested industrial zone. The TITANESS architecture solves the "Terrible Problem" of space debris not merely as a sanitation effort, but as the foundation for the first **Off-World Industrial Monopoly**. 

By integrating sentient neural software (NDOS), cryptographic legal governance (MdEC), advanced aerospace robotics (SDEC), and zero-G manufacturing (Space Faktory), TITANESS bypasses the gravity well's "Mass Paradox." We do not bring trash down to Earth; we tokenize it, smelt it, and print the next generation of space infrastructure directly in orbit.

**3. The Muscle: Space Debris Excellence Consortium (SDEC)**
The physical fleet and neutral international utility. Governed by a non-aggression protocol, the SDEC fleet executes the physical capture, containment, and recycling of orbital assets.

## PART II: THE LEGAL & ECONOMIC FRAMEWORK

*   **The Sovereign Hand-Over ("Protective Retrieval"):** Under OST Article VIII, touching a defunct sovereign satellite is legally considered piracy. We bypass this by logging all captures as emergency "Protective Retrievals"—akin to maritime salvage of hazardous wrecks.
*   **Blind Verification (Zero-Knowledge Proofs):** To process classified military assets ("Spy Satellites") without triggering geopolitical conflict, debris is locked in shielded vaults (TCUs). Utilizing DARPA-compliant SIEVE architecture, international observers verify the mass and origin of the vault's contents via MdEC metadata *without ever opening the box*. Nations pay a "Salvage Fee" to have their secrets returned un-probed.
*   **The Hardware-in-the-Loop (HIL) Safety Gate:** To prevent accidental kinetic events, AI cannot authorize a capture. The system uses an **Amber-to-Cyan** protocol. The flight computer (Amber/Observation) requires an RSA-2048 signed "GNC-ENABLE" packet from a human operator to trip a Solid State Relay, powering the thrusters and turning the hull indicators Cyan (Action/Acquire).

## PART IV: THE TITANESS SPACE FAKTORY (THE EVOLUTION)

**Document ID:** SDEC-MFG-FAKTORY-01
**Role:** Closed-Loop Orbital 3D Printing & Spacecraft Foundry

The Space Faktory is the culmination of the Phoenix Protocol. Launched via heavy-lift vehicles (SpaceX Starship), it is a brutalist, zero-G industrial megastructure featuring massive glowing graphene thermal radiators and an exposed truss spine. 

### The Manufacturing Modules:
1.  **The Maw (Ingestion & Triage):** The universal docking hub where Haulers drop off TCUs.
2.  **The Crucible (Zero-G Smelting):** Utilizes Electromagnetic Levitation Smelting (ELS) to suspend and melt aluminum/titanium in a vacuum, avoiding crucible contamination. It uses magnetic centrifugal force to separate pure metal from useless slag.
3.  **The Loom (3D Printing Foundry):** The manufacturing hub where the fleet "eats" dead satellites to print new ones.
4.  **The Oasis (Propellant Depot):** A solar-powered Water Electrolysis Plant that splits water-ice into Hydrogen and Oxygen, serving as a green "gas station" for the fleet.

### The Faktory Robotic Arsenal:
*   **Vulcan Stripper "Mantis-Arms":** 7-axis precision arms with Micro-Gravitational Containment Shrouds. They deploy a localized vacuum bubble to catch micro-debris while laser-cutting high-value parts (CPUs, solar arrays) prior to smelting.
*   **Aegis Siphon-Drones ("The Vampires"):** Autonomous crawler drones that attach to dead hulls, locate fuel lines via acoustic resonance, and "hot tap" the pipes to safely drain highly explosive, toxic hydrazine.
*   **Leviathan Electromagnetic Tethers ("The Anvil"):** Smart tension cables that fire out, wrap around structural hard points of a tumbling 10-ton satellite, and magnetically reel it tight against the Faktory deck to immobilize it.
*   **Hephaestus Print-Heads:** 6-axis arms equipped with Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS) wire-feeders. They feed solid recycled metal wire into a 5kW fiber laser, 3D-printing new spacecraft frames in zero gravity.
*   **MdEC QA-Wands:** Inspection arms that run ultrasonic/X-ray sweeps over newly printed parts to check for thermal stress fractures before using a laser to etch the new "Titaness-Certified" M-ID into the metal.
